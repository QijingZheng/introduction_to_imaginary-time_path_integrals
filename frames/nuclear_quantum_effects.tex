  \frametitle{Nuclear Quantum Effects}

  The \emph{thermal} de Broglie wavelength of a particle:
  $$
  \Lambda = \frac{h}{\sqrt{2\pi m k_B T}}
  $$
  % ase.units._hplanck / np.sqrt(
  %   2 * np.pi * ase.units._mp * ase.units.kB * T * ase.units._e
  % )
  for a hydrogen atom at \SI{300}{\kelvin}, $\Lambda \approx \SI{1.0}{\angstrom}$.
